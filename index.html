About me

A short resume

I'm a lecture teacher at Campus Monterrey and Santa Cataring, I teach computer science classes most of the time teaching Java and Labview. I'm also an expert programming PLCs and Arduinos. Has some experience with HTML5 & CSS3 and a selflearner in Android and Javascript.

Currently I'm finishing (if it doesn't finish me first) my PhD in Information Technologies at Tecnologico de Monterrey, some of my publications are included in this webpage.
